The eleventh UN sustainable developement goal is to ensure sustainable cities and communities. This could be energy sources, sustainable growth, and sufficient food and water sources for urban communities. 50% of the worlds population live in urban communities, and those communities need to be able to comfortably host their population, which is why sustainable cities is one of the most important UN goals.

The main problem with sustainable cities comes from the fact that many politicians and mayors are unwilling to convert their city into a more sustainable manner, because of the price or the chance that they may not be re-elected. This is a major problem because if the leaders of cities aren't willing to change them, then the city population won't en either.
There are many possible solutions to increase sustainability of cities and urban areas. These can include investment in renewable energy sources, investment and increase in public transit, insolation in buildings to keep heat, and investment in education. These are just some of the many ways we can keep our cities inhabitable and sustainable for years to come.
